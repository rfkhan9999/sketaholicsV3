Get messy and let the colors fly Our Objective

Sketchaholic is a platform between you and talented local lesser-known but very passionate artists. Our society is full of passionate and highly cretive individuals who may eventually give up on their passion because we tend to measure ourselves just through social media likes. Our intention is to keep the children that run wild inside all of us at times alive, and never give up on doing something that has the power to make us happy.

If you fall in love with the artworks show your support by following the artists on the various social media, a lot of these featured artworks are also for sale so do not hesitate to get in touch with the artists.

Art Name: The Eagle of Dagestan

Category: Traditional

A fire burnt inside him since he was just a young boy. Trained & disciplined by the decorated athlete & war veteran Abdulmanap Nurmagomedov , a stern and uncompromising father , the boy was destined for greatness. When he burst into the MMA scene years later he was able to amass a total of 16 wins & 0 losses within a space of 3 years. They started calling him "The eagle" a fitting name for the sky was the only limit he knew. Years went by, and the awestruck world witnessed as the young eagle clawed & mauled his way up the ranks to became the undisputed champion in the largest MMA promotion organization of the world ,the UFC with an unbelieveable record of 27-0, but the biggest fight of his life was still waiting for him in the form of a fiery Irishman known as "The Notorious" ; the biggest star, the most dominant opponent the matchmakers could offer. A man who could tumble giants with clean blows from his left fist.The signature insults and bravado of the Irishman fueled a blistering rivalry which eventually exploded and unfortunate events followed but in the end when the two finally met "Notorious" tapped out in front of thousands & thousands of his raucous Irish countrymen as if he was just a helpless impala stuck in the clutches of a fierce hunting bear. A devoted follower of Islam "The eagle" remains humble unlike his archrival. He has come a long way from the hard bear-wrestling days of his youth and achieved everything he ever wished for through his hardwork, discipline and dedication but the everpresent values and down-to-earthness is what makes this man a true champion. A genuinely admireable figure who is undoubtably worthy to be looked up to and followed by millions & millions around the world.
